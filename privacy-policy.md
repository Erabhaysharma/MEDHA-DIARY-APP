# Privacy Policy for Medha

Last updated: June 2026

## Introduction
Medha ("we", "our", "app") is a personal AI 
diary companion. We are committed to protecting 
your personal information.

## Information We Collect
- Email address (for account creation)
- Display name (chosen by you)
- Diary entries (written by you)
- Chat messages with AI companion

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide AI-powered diary features
- To personalize your experience
- To enable chat with your AI companion
- We NEVER sell your data to third parties

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using:
- Supabase (encrypted database)
- Pinecone (vector search, anonymized)

## AI Processing
Your diary entries are processed by:
- Google Gemini (for understanding context)
- Groq AI (for generating responses)
- Your data is never used to train AI models

## Data Security
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Passwords are never stored in plain text
- PIN lock available for extra privacy

## Your Rights
You can:
- Delete your account anytime
- Request your data deletion
- Export your diary entries

## Contact Us
For privacy concerns contact:
your@email.com

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. 
Users will be notified of major changes.
